About

I was first introduced to programming in a high school computer animation class. The class involved using Flash to create animations, but I stumbled upon ActionScript and quickly became fascinated. I have always loved puzzles and problem solving and programming is rife with both, along with an endless amount of learning and discovery.

I earned a computer science degree at Seattle Pacific University then worked at GarageGames first as a tools development intern and ultimately advancing to a programming lead. Since leaving GarageGames I have been self-employed, working primarily with Keuilian, inc as a full stack developer, and releasing a few games as a co-founder of PiRhoSoft.

Through my professional work and hobbyist projects I have been exposed to a wide variety of tools, languages, frameworks, etc. but I most enjoy and have the most experience with C++ and C#. I love tackling challenging problems that require creative solutions as well as creating tools and libraries that simplify challenging tasks.

Outside of work I enjoy playing and following sports, traveling, video games, reading, and pretty much anything where I can learn or experience something new.
